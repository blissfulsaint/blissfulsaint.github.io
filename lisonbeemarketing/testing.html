Split-Testing Additional Proof and Sales Copy on Landing Page vs. Super Simple Page

Implementation:

Many times, the simplest pages work extremely well for conversions, but not in all cases.

In fact, some industries and verticals depend heavily on proof and further convincing.

This depends on a few different factors such as:

  • Price Point
  • Industry
  • Positioning
  • Competition
  • Funnel Type

The only way to know is to test it.

Run a split-test of 50/50 traffic to your opt-in page. One variation will be your optin page with the pre-headline, headline, subheadline, key bullets and a simple opt-in box or button.

The other variation will be more evidencebased. It will have the same elements as the simple page, with additional proof elements such as testimonials, quotes, case studies and videos to show that the opt-in is real and produces results.

Notes:

You can use a tool such as www.useproof.com to easily embed proof into your pages and increase conversions.
